LOCKPICKING MECHANICS

Objective: Successfully pick all pins in the lock

Controls: Press SPACEBAR when a pin is in the green target zone

Failure: Miss the target zone and all pins reset

Success: All pins locked in correct positions

VISUAL ELEMENTS

Lock Body: Rectangular frame with corner decorations

Pin Housings: Vertical rectangles (static)

Pins: Moving gray rectangles that oscillate up/down

Target Zones: Green rectangular zones (random positions)

Pick Pointer: White triangle indicating current pin

Visual Feedback: Pins turn green when close to target zone

DIFFICULTY SCALING

Routine: 3 pins, 40px zones, slow speed

Standard: 4 pins, 30px zones, medium speed

Challenging: 5 pins, 20px zones, fast speed

Dangerous: 6 pins, 15px zones, very fast speed

CREW SKILL INTEGRATION

Calculation: speed * (1 - (skill/100 * 0.4))
